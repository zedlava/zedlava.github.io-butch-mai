Our Love Story

Jemima "Mai" Segui

The Bride

Jemima is a passionate accountant and an inspiring team leader, with a heart that beats for music and a life devoted to serving God. Her grace, strength, and unwavering faith have touched everyone around her—but none more than Arnold, who knew from the moment they met that she was the greatest blessing he would ever receive.

Arnold "Butch" Valdez

The Groom

Arnold is a devoted government employee and a passionate freelance software developer, but his greatest passion has always been Mai. During a serene getaway in Siruma, surrounded by the beauty of the sea and sky, he told her there was only one view more breathtaking than the horizon—her—and asked her to be his forever.

It all began on a golden morning by the sea, when we were introduced through someone dear to us beneath a sunrise that felt like destiny. Four years, countless adventures, and endless laughter later, we’re ready to begin the rest of our lives together.
